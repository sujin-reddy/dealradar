Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

DealRadar is built on a simple principle: we exist to save you money, not to sell your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how it's used — in plain language.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the service:

  • Search queries — the product names or URLs you search for, used to fetch comparison results.
  • Coupon submissions — if you submit a coupon code, we store the platform, code, description and votes.
  • Account information (optional) — if you create an account, we store your email address and encrypted password to enable saved wishlists and price alerts.
  • Usage data — basic analytics like page views and search counts to understand how DealRadar is used. No personally identifiable information is attached to this.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your browsing history outside of DealRadar.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.
  • We do not use your data to build advertising profiles.
  • We do not store payment information of any kind.

3. Affiliate Links and Third-Party Platforms

DealRadar participates in affiliate programmes run by Amazon, Flipkart, eBay, Nykaa and others. When you click a "Buy Now" button on our site and make a purchase, those platforms may set their own cookies to track the referral and pay us a commission.

These platforms have their own privacy policies which govern what data they collect when you visit their sites. We recommend reviewing their policies before making a purchase. The commission we earn does not affect the price you pay.

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences (e.g., saved searches, wishlist items stored locally)
  • Keep you signed in if you create an account
  • Basic analytics (anonymous page view counts)

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

5. Local Storage

DealRadar uses your browser's local storage to save your search count, saved coupons, and wishlist items locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser unless you create an account and choose to sync it.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect any data you share with us. Account passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. We do not store sensitive financial information.

7. Children's Privacy

DealRadar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email of any significant changes. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via our contact page. We respond to all privacy inquiries within 48 hours.

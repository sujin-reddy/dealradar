How We Make Money

Honest about how this works

DealRadar is completely free to use. We make money through affiliate commissions — when you click a "Buy Now" button and make a purchase on a platform like Amazon or Flipkart, that platform pays us a small percentage of the sale. You pay absolutely nothing extra. The price you see is the same price you'd pay going directly to the platform. We are just rewarded for sending them a buyer who was ready to purchase. This model means our incentive is always to show you the genuinely best deal — because you only buy when the price is right.

The Team

Who built this

DealRadar is an independent project built with one goal: save people money. We are not funded by any platform, brand, or advertiser. No platform pays us to rank higher or appear first — results are always sorted by price for the user's benefit.
